Intro

Hello and Welcome to my Portfolio. I am Game Developer and Designer with over 1 year of professional game industry experience and over 2.5 years of experience in Unity Game Engine. While I've worked on a wide variety of different mobile games.

Apart from the scripting part, I also design Game UIs. I'm a Unity Developer but also I'm moving my hands onto other softwares such as Ureal Engine, Figma, Photoshop and other designing tools. Mostly I've worked on Mobile Games of categories such as Hypercasual, Arcade, Action, Adventure, etc. I also love to do Level Design. By the way, check out my awesome work.

Work

Currently, I work mostly on mobile games of categories Hypercasual, Arcade, Action, etc. I focus on Gameplay Programming and I also capable of making Game UIs on my own. About

My name is Shreyash Patel. I'm a Computer Engineer graduated from the Sarvajanik College of Engineering and Technology.
